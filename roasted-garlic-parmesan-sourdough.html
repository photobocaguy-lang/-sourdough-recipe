Steps

Baker's notes

Starter readiness. Feed your starter 4 to 12 hours before mixing. It should be domed and just starting to fall. If it floats in water, it is ready.
Hydration. This dough runs at 71% hydration. Use wet hands during folding. Do not add extra flour to compensate.
Roasted garlic. Use whole squeezed cloves, not minced. They stay intact in the crumb and give you soft garlic pockets throughout the loaf.
Dutch oven. It must be fully preheated before the loaf goes in. A cold Dutch oven kills your oven spring.
Cool before cutting. Wait the full hour. The crumb is still setting as steam escapes. Cutting early gives you gummy bread.
Storage. Store cut-side down on a wooden board for up to 2 days. Do not refrigerate, it stales faster cold.
Flour swap. Replace up to 50g of bread flour with whole wheat for a nuttier flavor that pairs well with the roasted garlic.
